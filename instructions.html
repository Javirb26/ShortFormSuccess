Before you make your first video

You need to know these tips

Make A Bank Of Videos:

AT LEAST:

10 but preferabllt 20-30 before posting the first one

Try making 3 videos a day

DO NOT ATTEMPT EVERY EDITING STEP ALL AT ONCE

Start EASY

Just grab a clip, cut it, and practice making the audio coherent.

Then after 3 clips of practice Try to add a transition

After that try to add subtitles to another video etc.

YOU NEED TO PRACTICE ONE BY ONE BECAUSE YOU'RE FIRST VIDEO ISN'T GOING TO BE GREAT

All you need to do is practice the fundamentals seperately than and only them move forward.
